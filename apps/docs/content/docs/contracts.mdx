# Contracts

The Contracts module stores employment and vendor contract records. It is primarily used to track expiry dates and ensure renewals don't fall through the gaps.

## Contract records

Each contract contains:
- **Staff member** — linked to a staff profile
- **Contract type** — employment, vendor, SLA, maintenance, or other
- **Start and end date**
- **Status** — `active`, `expired`, `terminated`, or `pending`
- **Notes** — any relevant terms or conditions

## Expiring soon

The **Expiring Soon** view shows contracts whose end date falls within the next 60 days. Review this regularly — contract lapses can cause access issues and legal exposure.

When a contract expires, the associated staff profile should be transitioned to `inactive` (for employment contracts) or the vendor access should be reviewed in Platform Accounts.

## Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `active` | Contract is current |
| `expired` | Past end date, not renewed |
| `terminated` | Ended early |
| `pending` | Awaiting signature or start date |

## Integration with staff deactivation

When deactivating a staff member, check their contract record first. If the contract is still `active`, mark it as `terminated` to reflect the actual end of employment.

## Access

Contract records are accessible to `staff`, `manager`, `hrAdminOps`, and `admin` roles (they can view). Only `hrAdminOps` and `admin` can create or update contracts.
