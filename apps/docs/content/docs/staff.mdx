# Staff Directory

The Staff Directory is the central record of everyone on the DCS team. Every other module (leave, work items, roster, compliance) references a staff profile.

## Staff profiles

Each profile contains:
- **Name, email, job title, phone**
- **Department** — assigned DCS sub-unit (Network Services, Core Infrastructure, etc.)
- **Employment type** — `permanent`, `contract`, or `consultant`
- **Status** — `active`, `on_leave`, `inactive`
- A link to their Better Auth user account (for login)

## Creating staff

Staff accounts are created by admins/HR Ops via:
1. **Manual creation** — Staff Directory → New Staff
2. **Bulk import** — Import Data → staff CSV upload

Self-signup is disabled. Staff cannot create their own accounts.

## Departments

Departments group staff for reporting and filtering. Current DCS departments:
- Network Services (ASN)
- Core Infrastructure
- Security
- Support

Departments are managed under **Settings → Departments**.

## Status transitions

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `active` | Currently working |
| `on_leave` | On approved leave (auto-updated when leave is approved) |
| `inactive` | Departed, contract ended, or suspended |

## Deactivating staff

Use **Deactivate** (not delete) when a staff member leaves. This preserves all their historical records (work items, leave history, audit trail) while preventing login. Deactivated accounts are excluded from roster eligibility and leave balance calculations.

## Workload view

The **Workload View** shows every active staff member's current load:
- Open work items (count)
- Overdue work items
- Current on-call role (if any)
- Whether they're on leave today
- An overall **load score** and **load level** (low / medium / high / critical)

Use this when assigning new work to find the least-loaded engineer.

## HR records linked to staff

Each staff profile is the anchor for:
- Leave balances and requests
- Contract records
- Appraisals
- Training and PPE compliance records
- Platform accounts (VPN, Zabbix, etc.)
