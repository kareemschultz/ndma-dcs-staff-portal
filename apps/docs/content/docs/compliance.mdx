# Compliance

The Compliance module tracks health and safety obligations for DCS staff.

## Training records

Log all mandatory and optional training courses completed by staff. Track expiry dates for certifications that require renewal.

## PPE records

Record Personal Protective Equipment issued to staff — type, quantity, issue date, and condition.

## Policy acknowledgements

Staff acknowledge policies (OSH, data protection, acceptable use, etc.) through the system. Track who has acknowledged each policy and when.

## Expiry alerts

The system surfaces items expiring within 30 days — training certifications, PPE requiring inspection, etc. — on the dashboard and in the Compliance module.
