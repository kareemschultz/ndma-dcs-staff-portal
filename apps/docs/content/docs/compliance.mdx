# Compliance

The Compliance module tracks health, safety, and regulatory obligations for all DCS staff. It replaces spreadsheet-based tracking for training certifications, PPE issuance, and policy acknowledgement records.

## Training records

Log all mandatory and optional training completed by staff.

Each training record contains:
- **Staff member** тАФ who attended
- **Training name** тАФ course or certification title
- **Provider** тАФ who delivered the training
- **Completion date** тАФ when it was completed
- **Expiry date** тАФ when re-certification is required (if applicable)
- **Status** тАФ `completed`, `expired`, or `pending`
- **Notes** тАФ certificate number, score, or other details

### Common training types tracked
- OSH (Occupational Safety and Health) induction
- Fire safety and evacuation
- Data centre operations certification
- Cybersecurity awareness
- Vendor-specific certifications (Huawei, Cisco, etc.)

## PPE records

Record Personal Protective Equipment issued to staff.

Each PPE record contains:
- **Staff member** тАФ who received the equipment
- **Item** тАФ type of PPE (e.g., safety shoes, hi-vis vest, helmet, gloves)
- **Quantity** тАФ number of items issued
- **Issue date** тАФ when it was given out
- **Condition** тАФ `new`, `used`, or `damaged`
- **Notes** тАФ serial numbers or special notes

PPE records help DCS confirm that all staff operating in the data centre have appropriate protective equipment and that issued items are tracked.

## Policy acknowledgements

Staff must formally acknowledge key operational and HR policies. Each acknowledgement records:
- **Staff member** тАФ who acknowledged
- **Policy name** тАФ e.g., "Acceptable Use Policy", "Data Protection Policy", "OSH Policy"
- **Acknowledged at** тАФ timestamp of acknowledgement
- **Notes** тАФ any exceptions or comments

To acknowledge a policy, a staff member navigates to the Compliance section and clicks **Acknowledge** on a pending policy. Acknowledgements are permanent and auditable.

## Record statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| `active` | Current and valid |
| `expired` | Past the expiry date тАФ action required |
| `pending` | Scheduled but not yet completed |

## Expiry alerts

The system surfaces items expiring within 30 days in two places:
1. **Compliance module** тАФ the **Expiring Items** view
2. **Dashboard** тАФ the ops readiness panel flags expired training and PPE gaps

Review this view weekly to avoid compliance lapses. When a certification expires, the staff member and their manager receive an in-app notification.

## Adding a record

All three record types (training, PPE, policy acknowledgement) are managed by `hrAdminOps` or `admin` roles. Staff can view their own records but cannot create or modify them directly.

To add a training record:
1. Navigate to **Compliance тЖТ Training**
2. Click **Add Training Record**
3. Select the staff member and fill in course details
4. Set an expiry date if the certification has a renewal period

## Bulk view

The main Compliance page shows all records across training, PPE, and policy acknowledgements with filters by staff member, status, and type. Use the **Expiring Items** tab to prioritise renewal actions.
